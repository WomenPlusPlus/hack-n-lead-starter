# 1. Your Repo

* [ ] Make it public
* [ ] Open Source it (use GPL v3, MIT etc)

# 2. Your Work

Do things with your team!

Checklist:
* [ ] Hack
* [ ] Be at Hack'n'Lead

# 3. Presentation

Make your presentation and/or walkthrough

* [ ] Attach to form!

* [ ] Add presentation

* [ ] Submit!
